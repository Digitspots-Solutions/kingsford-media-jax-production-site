Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to apply?

You need a valid government-issued ID (Driver's License, National ID, Voter's Card, or International Passport), your BVN, and an active bank account.

How long does approval take?

Most applications are processed and approved within 5-10 minutes. Complex cases may take up to 24 hours.

What are the interest rates?

Our interest rates range from 5% to 15% per month depending on loan amount, tenure, and your credit profile. All rates are clearly displayed before you accept any loan.

Can I repay my loan early?

Yes! Early repayment is encouraged and there are no prepayment penalties. You may even qualify for reduced interest.

What if I can't repay on time?

Contact our customer support immediately. We offer flexible restructuring options to help you manage your repayment without penalties when possible.

Is my information secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption and comply with CBN regulations and data protection standards to keep your information safe.

What is the maximum loan amount?

Personal loans go up to ₦500,000. Business loans can reach ₦1,000,000. Your eligibility depends on your credit assessment.

Do I need collateral?

No. Our microloans are unsecured. We use advanced credit scoring technology instead of requiring physical collateral.
